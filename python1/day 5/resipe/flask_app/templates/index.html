Spam Musubi

Posted by: Kai

Description: A delicious Hawaiian favorite.

Under 30 Minutes? Yes

Instructions: Cook the rice. Pan fry several slices of spam. When rice and spam are done, distribute a row of rice a half-inch from the edge of the sushi paper. Layer spam on top. Roll, and enjoy!

Date Made: January 9th, 2001
